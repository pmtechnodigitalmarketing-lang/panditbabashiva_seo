========================================
6-MONTH SEO EXECUTION PLAN (STEP-BY-STEP)
========================================

This document explains how to execute SEO daily and weekly in a practical way.
Follow this process consistently for 6 months to see strong ranking and traffic growth.

----------------------------------------
DAILY SEO ROUTINE (5–15 MINUTES)
----------------------------------------

Every day, start with a quick SEO health check.

First, check your keyword rankings using tools like Ahrefs or SERP Robot.
Look at your main keywords and see if any have dropped in position.
If you notice a drop, mark that keyword and page for optimization later.

Next, open Google Search Console and check clicks and impressions.
If clicks are decreasing but impressions are stable, it means your CTR is low.
This indicates that your title or meta description needs improvement.

Then, check for new backlinks in Ahrefs.
If you gained new links, review their quality.
If they are good links, note what strategy worked so you can repeat it.

After that, go to the Coverage section in Google Search Console.
Check if there are any indexing or crawl errors.
Fix these immediately because they can directly affect rankings.

Now check your leads or conversions in GA4 or your CRM.
Track how many calls, form submissions, or conversions came from organic traffic.
This helps you understand if SEO is generating real business results.

Finally, check a few competitor keywords.
If competitors are improving their rankings, analyze what they changed.

----------------------------------------
WEEKLY SEO EXECUTION PLAN
----------------------------------------

Each week has a specific focus. Repeat this cycle every month.

----------------------------------------
WEEK 1: DATA ANALYSIS AND PLANNING
----------------------------------------

Start the week by analyzing your SEO performance.

Check your keyword rankings and focus on your top 20 important keywords.
See which keywords improved and which dropped.

Then go to Google Search Console and analyze queries.
Look for keywords that have high impressions but low CTR.
These are easy opportunities to gain more traffic by improving titles.

Next, identify pages that are getting impressions but not clicks.
Update their meta titles and descriptions to make them more attractive.

Analyze competitor backlinks using Ahrefs.
Find where they are getting links from and create a list of sites for outreach.

Check your internal linking structure using Screaming Frog.
Ensure important pages (like service pages) are getting enough internal links.

Finally, plan what content needs to be updated or created this month.

----------------------------------------
WEEK 2: CONTENT OPTIMIZATION
----------------------------------------

This week is focused on improving your existing content.

Start by updating at least 2 blog posts.
Improve content quality, add keywords, and increase content depth.

Add FAQ sections to your pages.
This helps you rank for featured snippets and increases visibility.

Improve headings (H1, H2, H3) using semantic keywords.
Make sure your content matches search intent.

Add internal links from blogs to your main service or pillar pages.
This improves authority flow across your site.

Optimize all images by compressing them and adding ALT text with keywords.
This helps with image SEO and page speed.

----------------------------------------
WEEK 3: BACKLINK BUILDING
----------------------------------------

This week focuses on increasing your website authority.

Start outreach for guest posting.
Send emails to relevant websites in your niche asking for content collaboration.

Build niche edit backlinks by finding relevant articles and getting your links added.

Submit your website to directories and citation sites.
This is especially important for local SEO.

Answer questions on platforms like Quora and Reddit.
Provide helpful answers and include your website link where relevant.

Check all backlinks you gained.
Make sure they are from good quality sites (high DR).

----------------------------------------
WEEK 4: SERP OPTIMIZATION AND GROWTH
----------------------------------------

This week is focused on improving click-through rate and visibility.

Start by optimizing meta titles.
Make them more engaging and include power words.

Update meta descriptions to improve CTR.
Add clear benefits and calls-to-action.

Search for featured snippet opportunities.
Try to structure your content in a way that answers questions clearly.

Add FAQ schema to pages.
This helps your content appear as rich results in Google.

Finally, review your organic traffic in GA4.
Check if traffic is increasing and which pages are performing best.

----------------------------------------
MONTHLY CONTENT STRATEGY
----------------------------------------

Each month, follow this publishing plan:

Week 1: Publish 1 new blog post targeting a primary keyword  
Week 2: Update 2 old blog posts  
Week 3: Publish 1 cluster article (supporting content)  
Week 4: Update your main pillar page  

----------------------------------------
SIMPLE WEEKLY WORKFLOW
----------------------------------------

Monday:
Analyze rankings and review Google Search Console data.

Tuesday:
Update and improve existing content.

Wednesday:
Work on internal linking.

Thursday:
Focus on backlink outreach.

Friday:
Optimize titles, descriptions, and SERP appearance.

Saturday:
Publish new blog content.

Sunday:
Review overall SEO performance and plan next week.

----------------------------------------
FINAL RESULT AFTER 6 MONTHS
----------------------------------------

If you follow this process consistently:

- Your keyword rankings will improve (Top 10 positions)
- Organic traffic will increase steadily
- Your website authority will grow
- You will generate more leads and conversions
- Your SEO process will become predictable and scalable
