 City Pages SEO Update – Implementation Guide

 Project: Pandit Baba Shiva Website
 Task: City Page Optimization (Content + Reviews)


 Objective
Improve SEO performance, indexing, and user trust by:
- Creating unique content for each city page
- Adding real-style Google reviews/testimonials
- Matching competitor-level page quality

---
 1. City Page Content Update

Requirements:
- Each city page must have **100% unique content**
- No duplicate paragraphs across pages
- Minimum **1000–1500 words per page**

 Content Structure:
1. H1 – Service + City  
   Example:  
   `Black Magic Removal in Boston`

2. Introduction  
   - Mention city-specific problems
   - Natural keyword usage

3. Main Content Sections:
   - Problem explanation (city-based context)
   - Astrology/spiritual solution
   - Benefits of service
   - Why choose us

4. Keyword Usage:
   - Use primary keyword 5–7 times naturally
   - Avoid keyword stuffing
