SEO KEYWORD RESEARCH, TITLE & META DESCRIPTION IMPLEMENTATION

Overview:
Completed SEO keyword research along with optimized page titles and meta descriptions for the website. The structure follows SEO best practices to improve rankings, CTR, and conversions.

- Keywords categorized into:
  - Home Page (short-tail + brand keywords)
  - Service Pages (transactional + high-conversion keywords)

Implementation Guidelines:

- Ensure content is user-friendly and SEO optimized
