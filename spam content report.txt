SEO Content Issues & Update Requirements

Summary:
During the SEO audit, multiple content issues were identified across service pages. These need to be fixed to improve rankings, avoid spam signals, and enhance content quality.

Key Issues:

1. Keyword Over-Optimization
- Repeated use of similar keywords (e.g., negative energy, healing, emotional imbalance)
- Content appears over-optimized and unnatural

2. Repetitive Content
- Multiple pages have similar structure and wording
- Low uniqueness across pages (Health, Spiritual, Relationship, etc.)

3. Low Content Value
- Generic and repetitive sentences
- Lack of meaningful or actionable insights

4. Lack of Expertise (E-E-A-T)
- Missing astrology-specific details (planets, houses, dashas)
- No strong trust signals or disclaimers

5. Weak FAQ Sections
- Generic questions
- Not targeting long-tail or search intent keywords

6. Image ALT Over-Optimization
- Keyword-stuffed ALT tags

Required Actions:

- Rewrite content to make each page unique
- Reduce keyword repetition
- Add specific astrology insights (Moon, Venus, Saturn, Rahu, houses, etc.)
- Improve headings and structure
- Optimize FAQ sections with targeted keywords
- Add disclaimer for trust and compliance
- Use natural, user-focused language instead of SEO-heavy content
